[02]Frontier · R&D In development. Shown for ceiling, not completion.
BioAnalyst - multi-agent hypothesis stress-tester

It tells you when your science doesn't hold.

You hand it a research proposal. It extracts the falsifiable claims, retrieves evidence across four federated literature databases, runs deterministic statistical tests on real gene-set data, and issues a structured verdict - with a skeptic agent whose only job is to try to break the result.

The architectural opinion is the point: the LLM formalizes, plans, and critiques; it computes nothing. Every statistic runs in scipy/statsmodels, then a leave-one-out pass checks whether significance survives dropping each gene.

The skill isn't "use AI." It's knowing where AI must not be trusted - and building the seam so the result stays auditable.
